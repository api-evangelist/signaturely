Managing contracts is a crucial aspect of running any business. Most companies regularly use contracts to enter new partnerships, sell products, and outline the terms of important agreements. Unfortunately, constantly managing dozens, hundreds, or even thousands of contracts can be a frustrating and exhausting experience for legal teams if you don’t do it properly.
